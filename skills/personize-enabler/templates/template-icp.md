# Ideal Customer Profile

**Description:** Three-tier ICP defining target segments for outreach prioritization.

**Tags:** `icp`, `sales`, `targeting`

---

## Tier 1 -- Ideal Fit
- Industry: [e.g., B2B SaaS, Healthcare]
- Company size: [e.g., 50-500 employees]
- Revenue: [e.g., $5M-$50M ARR]
- Role: [e.g., VP Sales, CRO, Head of RevOps]
- Signals: [e.g., hiring for sales roles, recent funding]
MUST prioritize Tier 1 for all outreach campaigns.

## Tier 2 -- Good Fit
- Industry: [adjacent industries]
- Company size: [e.g., 20-50 or 500-2000]
- Role: [e.g., Director level, Sales Manager]
SHOULD include Tier 2 when Tier 1 pipeline is healthy.

## Tier 3 -- Opportunistic
- Matches some criteria but not all
- Inbound interest or referral
MAY engage Tier 3 reactively, MUST NOT proactively prospect.

## Qualification Flow
Contact identified -> check Tier match -> Tier 1: immediate outreach -> Tier 2: queue -> Tier 3: only if inbound
