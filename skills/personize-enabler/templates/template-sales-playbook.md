# Sales Playbook

**Description:** Stage-specific messaging, objection handling, and engagement rules.

**Tags:** `sales`, `outbound`, `playbook`

---

## Prospecting Stage
MUST research before reaching out -- because generic outreach damages brand.
SHOULD reference a specific trigger (funding, hiring, product launch).
Cadence: 3 touches over 10 days (email, email, LinkedIn).

## Discovery Stage
MUST ask about pain points before pitching -- because premature solutions lose deals.
Key questions: What's your current process? What triggered this evaluation? Who else is involved?

## Proposal Stage
MUST include ROI estimate -- because budget holders need numbers.
SHOULD provide 2-3 pricing options.

## Objection Handling
| Objection | Response Pattern |
|-----------|-----------------|
| "Too expensive" | Reframe to cost of inaction |
| "Not the right time" | Agree, set future follow-up |
| "Using competitor X" | Ask what's missing, focus on gaps |
| "Need to check with team" | Offer to join the internal meeting |
