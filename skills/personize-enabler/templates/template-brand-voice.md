# Brand Voice Guidelines

**Description:** Defines tone, vocabulary, and communication style for all AI-generated content.

**Tags:** `brand`, `content`, `generation`

---

## Tone
MUST use a [professional/conversational/consultative] tone -- because consistent voice builds trust.
SHOULD match formality to the channel: emails formal, Slack casual, docs neutral.

## Vocabulary
MUST use: [solution, partnership, insight, enable]
MUST NOT use: [synergy, leverage, disrupt, game-changing]

## Examples

GOOD: "Here's how this helps your team move faster."
BAD: "Leverage our game-changing solution to disrupt your workflow."

## Channel Formatting
- Email: greeting, 2-3 short paragraphs, clear CTA
- Slack: concise, emoji OK, link to detail
- Blog: scannable headers, examples, 800-1200 words
